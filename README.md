 Vehicle Service Center Management System (Web & Mobile)

A full-stack web and mobile application developed for **Ananda Auto Motor Service, Kurunegala**, to digitalize and streamline vehicle service center operations. The system provides customers with an easy appointment booking experience while enabling workshop staff to efficiently manage daily operations through a real-time admin dashboard.

 Project Overview

Ananda Auto Motor Service operates with **10 vehicle service slots**. This system was designed to reduce manual record keeping and improve customer experience by providing online booking, service tracking, and business analytics.

Problem Statement 

Many vehicle service centers still use manual systems (paper records, phone calls, Excel) to manage bookings and customer data, which leads to booking conflicts, missed appointments, and poor tracking of service history.

At Ananda Auto Motor Service, managing only 10 service slots efficiently and handling customer appointments manually created delays, errors, and poor customer communication.

The system was built to digitize and automate bookings, improve scheduling efficiency, and provide real-time access to appointments, service history, and reminders.
